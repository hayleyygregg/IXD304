Monstercons

by Hayley Gregg

Monstercons are a set of furry friends from around the world. They live on planet Earth with us humans and have done for years, we just havn't seen them - until now!

These little creatures were discovered by Scientist Clarice Fuller when she was cleaning her microscope. You see, Monstercons are so tiny they are invisable to the human eye!

No need to fear, Monstercons are no threat to us - they want to be our friends! Clarice is working on finding a way for us to be able to see the monsters in real life to make it easier for us to communicate. Until then she has provided details about each monster from her discovery so we can get to know them!

Miley

  • Age: 3
  • Gender: Male
  • Origin: England

Miley is seen as the leader of the group. He was the first monstercon to be discovered by Clarice, introducing her to his other monster friends. He is a happy, bubbly character that likes to make friends.

Nina

  • Age: 2 ½
  • Gender: Female
  • Origin: Spain

Nina is originally from Spain, she came to England in search of creatures like her where she met Miley. She likes to dance and is known for taking her daily siestas.

Buddy

  • Age: 2
  • Gender: Male
  • Origin: England

Buddy was found by Miley and they soon became close friends. They see eachother as brothers. Buddy is a bit clumsy and (SOMETHING ELSE)

Viola

  • Age: 1 ½
  • Gender: Female
  • Origin: Italy

The Italian family Viola lived with migrated to England, she lost them while travelling but was found by Miley. She doesn't talk much and her favourite food is spaghetti and meatballs.

Rowan

  • Age: 2
  • Gender: Male
  • Origin: Ireland

Rowan is Irish, he came to visit England where he met Miley and the growing group of monsters. He became good friends with them and decided to stay. He likes potatoes.

Toro

  • Age: 2
  • Gender: Male
  • Origin: Spain

Toro is Spanish, he lived in Portugal, a country known for the sport of Bull fighting. Toro likes to pretend he is in a Bull fight by running around red things. He came to England in search of Nina and became part of the Monstercon family.

Haul

  • Age: 2 ½
  • Gender: Male
  • Origin: Wales

Haul is Welsh, he is a bright character that likes adventure. He decided to travel to England when he heard of creatures like him lived there.

Nur

  • Age: 6 months
  • Gender: Male
  • Origin: Israel

Nur is the youngest member of the group. He was found by Haul when he was on one of his adventures. The group believe he was born in England but his parents have not been found, so they decided to adopt him.

Ryuu

  • Age: 2 ½
  • Gender: Male
  • Origin: Japan

Ryuu used to live in Japan, he came to England in search of creatures like him and became part of the group. He is protective over Nur and sees him as a son. He likes Sushi and Fire Breathing.

Kimiko

  • Age: 1 ½
  • Gender: Female
  • Origin: Japan

Kimiko is also from Japan, she moved to England with the family she stayed with. She bumped into Ryuu who introduced her to the Monstercon family and she never left. Shes a shy, giggly character.

Bruno

  • Age: 2 ¾
  • Gender: Male
  • Origin: Germany

Bruno came to England as the family he stayed with were taking part in the continental market. He was accidently passed onto a customer when he was inside a bag, somehow he ended up bumping into the Monstercons to his delight and decided to stay with them.

Kayode

  • Age: 1 ¾
  • Gender: Male
  • Origin: Africa

Kayode is a little rascal, He's always getting into mischeif. One day he decided to go on an adventure and ended up on a flight to England. Luckily Miley and his friends found him, although they still have a hard time keeping up with him!
